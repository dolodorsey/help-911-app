Common Questions

How do I check my case status? Open the app, tap "Check My Case Status" on the home screen, and log in with your phone number.

How do I request a ride? Go to the Rides tab and tap "Request a Ride."

How do I upload documents? Go to the Documents tab and tap "Tap to Upload."
